Buying and modifying a project car has never been easier

A car is a symbol of freedom, and modifying a car is a great expression of that freedom. This site will guide you through the process of inspecting a project car, buying a project car, and some common modifications to improve the driving appearance and aesthetics of the project.

But why buy a project car?

Buying and modifying a project car is a labor of love. The end goal of a project car is to tailor the driving experience to your liking. That being said, there are quite a few pros to buying and modifying a project car.

  • Project cars are less expensive to purchase than a perfectly working vehicle.
  • There's so much to learn about how to repair and maintain a used car.
  • Project cars can be upgraded and modified as you have money, instead of making a very large down payment.
