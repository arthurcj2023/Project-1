Pricing guide

This pricing calculator should be able to give you an accurate value of the vehicle you are looking to purchase. Taking factors from the inspection portion of the site, rate the condition of each major category of the vehicle on a scale from 0-10. 0 is equivalent to scrap metal, and 10 is equivalent to fresh off of the factory line. Once you plug in those values press the Calculate Price button to get what we recommend you try and purchase the vehicle for. Lower scores in any category greatly impact our recommended price, as the worse a car is when you buy it, the more maintainence there is bound to be in the future.
